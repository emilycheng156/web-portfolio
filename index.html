About Me

Hi! I'm Emily Cheng, a recent graduate of the University of Maryland with degrees in Mathematics (Statistics) and Computer Science and a minor in General Business. I'm passionate about using data to solve real-world problems, combining statistical analysis, programming, and visualization to uncover insights that drive better decisions.

Through internships at Johnson & Johnson, MassMutual, and Ticketmaster, I've worked on projects ranging from pharmaceutical demand forecasting and insurance analytics to cloud-based monitoring systems. My experience includes forecasting, data visualization, statistical modeling, and analyzing large datasets to support business and operational decision-making.
